Welcome,
   

I am Vishnu Chipade, a PhD candidate in Aerospace Engineering department at the University of Michigan. Currently, I am working as Graduate Student Research Assistant in the Distributed Aerospace Systems and Control Laboratory at the University of Michigan.


My research interests are in the areas of motion planning and decision making for multi-agent systems, particularly aerial robots. I am also interested in applications of computer vision in aerial robotics.
